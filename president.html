The Club / Presidency

Edward Wycliffe

President, Islington Football Club

  • Founding chairman of Islington Football Club.
  • Leads the club's long-term sporting and institutional strategy.
  • Advocates youth development, responsible ownership, and community connection.

Edward Wycliffe is the founding chairman of Islington Football Club and the architect behind the club's long-term vision. Under his leadership, Islington FC has been built upon the principles of integrity, sustainability, and sporting excellence.

Born into a family with longstanding interests in international business and investment, Wycliffe has consistently advocated a patient and responsible approach to football ownership. Rather than pursuing short-term success, he believes in developing a modern club identity based on youth development, intelligent recruitment, financial discipline, and a strong connection with the local community.

His ambition is to establish Islington FC as one of England's most respected football institutions: a club capable of competing at the highest level while preserving the values of sportsmanship, elegance, and long-term stewardship.

Since assuming the chairmanship, Wycliffe has overseen significant investment in football operations, infrastructure, and community initiatives. The club's recent growth reflects his conviction that sustainable success is achieved not through excess, but through vision, stability, and thoughtful leadership.

Football clubs are not built for a single season. They are built for generations.

Edward Wycliffe
